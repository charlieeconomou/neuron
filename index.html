01

What is Neuron?

Neuron is a classroom-aligned AI approach for programming education that follows instructor-defined rules and emphasizes understanding over shortcuts.

For instructors

Set AI rules by assignment

Define what help is allowed and when — clearly and consistently.

For students

Guided help, not answers

Step-by-step support that teaches how to think, debug, and learn.

For courses

Transparency by design

Provide context when questions arise without “gotcha” monitoring.

02

Why this matters

AI is already part of the development world — education needs a learning-first way to use it responsibly.

  • Students get mixed messages about what AI is allowed, causing inconsistent use and confusion.
  • Large classes limit feedback — instructors can’t review every debugging attempt or question.
  • Departments want visibility into AI use without policing students or relying on detection tools.
